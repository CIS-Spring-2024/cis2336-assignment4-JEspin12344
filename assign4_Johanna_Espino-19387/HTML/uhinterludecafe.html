Welcome to Interlude Cafe!





At Interlude Cafe at the University of Houston in Sugarland, we provide
students with a space at which they can spend their time doing various
activities. Students can use Interlude Cafe as a break spot, a study spot, or
a place to hang out with friends.

Interlude Cafe holds many food options, from breakfast to dinner, to satisfy
your taste buds at all hours of the day. Don't be afraid to explore our spot
and its available menu!
