About Us

Our Founder

Johanna Espino is the owner of the newly formed Interlude Cafe at the University of Houston at Sugarland. From a young age, Johanna Espino wanted to open her own cafe so she made it a goal for the future.

As a University of Houston alumni, Johanna was inspired by the environment she got to be a part of for four years. On campus, there were plenty of spots for students to spend their time in and cafes were some of the most popular locations.

Johanna was given the opportunity to create a new cafe for the students at the University of Houston at Sugarland. The new Interlude Cafe is a perfect place for students to comfortably study, hang out with friends, or enjoy a break alone.

Thrilled to share the experience of Interlude Cafe with others, Johanna hopes students of the University of Houston will enjoy the new space created to fit their needs.
